Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how PDF Master Pro protects your data.

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PDF Master Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your documents. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our PDF processing tools.

Unlike most online PDF tools, PDF Master Pro processes all files locally in your browser. This means your documents never leave your computer, providing unparalleled privacy protection.

How We Protect Your Privacy

100% Browser-Based Processing

All PDF processing happens directly in your web browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

No File Uploads

When you select a file for processing, it stays entirely within your browser's memory. No data is transmitted over the internet after the initial page load.

Local Processing

All PDF operations (conversion, merging, splitting, etc.) are performed locally using client-side JavaScript libraries. Your data never leaves your device.

Automatic Cleanup

All file data is automatically cleared from browser memory when you: - Close the tab - Navigate away - Click the "Reset" button - Process completes

Minimal Cookies

We only use essential cookies for basic functionality. No tracking cookies or analytics that compromise your privacy.

What Data We Collect (And What We Don't)

What We Collect

  • Anonymous usage statistics (tool popularity)
  • Browser type and version (for compatibility)
  • Basic error reports (without file data)
  • Session preferences (your selected settings)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Your PDF files or document contents
  • Personal information or contact details
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Tracking cookies or analytics data

Technical Security Measures

Browser Sandbox Security

All processing occurs within your browser's security sandbox, which prevents any data from being accessed by other websites or applications.

Open Source Libraries

We use established, open-source PDF libraries (PDF-Lib, pdf.js) that have been extensively reviewed by the security community.

No Backend Processing

Our website has no backend server for file processing. The entire application runs client-side, eliminating server-side security risks.

Third-Party Services

PDF Master Pro uses the following third-party services that may collect anonymous data according to their respective privacy policies:

Google Fonts

Font delivery service

CDNJS

JavaScript library hosting

These services do not receive your PDF files or document contents. They only serve static assets like fonts and JavaScript libraries.

Your Responsibilities

Browser Security: Ensure your browser is up-to-date and secure. Older browsers may have security vulnerabilities.

Device Security: PDF Master Pro cannot protect against malware or keyloggers on your device. Use antivirus software.

Public Computers: Avoid using PDF Master Pro on public or shared computers where others might access your files.

Contact & Policy Updates

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our website.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top will indicate when changes were made. Continued use of PDF Master Pro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Privacy is Guaranteed

With PDF Master Pro, your documents stay on your device. We cannot access your files even if we wanted to - by design.
